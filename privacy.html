Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CallMinder ("the App") handles information.

1. Data You Provide

You may create reminder records, including names, phone numbers, and email addresses, to use reminder features.

2. Contacts Access

If you grant permission, the App can access contacts so you can pick people when creating reminders. Contacts access is optional and can be changed in iOS Settings.

3. Notifications

The App requests notification permission to deliver reminder alerts on your device.

4. Storage and Sync

  • Reminder data is stored locally on your device.
  • If you enable iCloud sync, data is synced to your private iCloud database (CloudKit).
  • We do not run our own remote server for your reminder content.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data and do not share your reminder content with third parties, except when required by law.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete reminders in the App. You can also disable iCloud sync and remove local data from within the App settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: mustafa@example.com
