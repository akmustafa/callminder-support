What You Can Do

Built for real follow-ups

Whether it is a quick family call, a client check-in, or recurring outreach, CallMinder keeps the next action visible and easy to complete.

1

Set reminders your way

Create reminders for voice calls, video calls, messages, and emails with one-time or recurring schedules.

2

Adjust when life moves

Snooze quickly, skip the next occurrence, or pause recurring reminders when the timing changes.

3

See what matters now

Track reminders across Scheduled, Pending, and Completed views so nothing gets lost.

4

Work from your contacts

Pick people faster from your address book and keep follow-ups tied to the right contact details.

5

Choose your default apps

Use the calling, video calling, and messaging apps that already fit your workflow.

6

Keep data safe

Sync with iCloud across Apple devices and move data with backup export and import.
